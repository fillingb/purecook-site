Is Your Air Fryer Toxic?

By Sarah Miller, PhD | March 11, 2026

Standard air fryers often use PTFE (Teflon) coatings that off-gas at high temperatures. We conducted a 450°F Sealed Chamber VOC Test on top-selling units to find the truly non-toxic alternatives.

🔬 The PureCook VOC Protocol

We ran each air fryer at 450°F inside a 1m³ sealed glass chamber for 4 continuous hours. Using a photoionization detector (PID), we monitored for Volatile Organic Compounds and fluoropolymer particulate matter linked to "Teflon flu."
