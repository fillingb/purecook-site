Ceramic vs. Granite: The Showdown

By Sarah Miller, PhD | March 11, 2026

If you're ditching Teflon, you've narrowed it down to two contenders: Ceramic and Granite/Stone. We put both through our 1,000 Scrape Robotic Arm Test to see which one truly survives the modern kitchen.

🔬 The PureCook Abrasion Protocol

Using a weighted 500g mechanical arm, we simulated 1,000 metal-spatula scrapes on each surface at 400°F. After the abrasion, we performed a "Dry Egg Release" test to measure how much non-stick performance was lost.

Top Pick: GreenPan Valencia Pro (Ceramic)

The GreenPan is our winner for high-heat performance. In our 600°F thermal overload test, the Thermolon™ coating remained structurally intact. It is the only ceramic pan that passed our "Metal Tool Survival" phase without visible micro-scratches.
