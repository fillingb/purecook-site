Ceramic vs. Granite: Which Non-Stick Pan Lasts Longer?

Updated February 15, 2026 | By PureCook Lab

If you're ditching Teflon, you've probably narrowed it down to two contenders: Ceramic (like Caraway or GreenPan) and Granite/Stone (like Carote). But what's the actual difference?

Spoiler Alert: "Granite" pans don't actually contain granite rock. It's a marketing term for a specific look of coating. Most are actually... ceramic (or PTFE reinforced with minerals).

At a Glance: The Breakdown

Feature Ceramic (Sol-Gel) Granite (Stone-derived)
Non-Toxic? ✅ 100% PFAS-Free ⚠️ Check label (some contain PTFE)
Durability Medium (1-2 years) High (2-4 years)
Heat Tolerance High (up to 800°F) Medium (usually 400°F)
Best For Eggs, Pancakes, Fish Everyday Heavy Cooking
