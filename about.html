Our Protocol

Science Over Marketing.

At PureCook Lab, we don't believe in "sponsored" health. We believe in molecular reality. Every recommendation on this site is the result of a multi-stage verification process designed to keep your kitchen free from endocrine disruptors and forever chemicals.

01

Anonymity

We buy every product anonymously at full retail price. We do not accept PR samples or "review units."

02

XRF Scanning

We use portable X-Ray Fluorescence to verify that ceramic glazes are truly 100% lead and cadmium free.

03

Off-Gassing

Products are heated in a controlled thermal chamber while air sensors monitor for PTFE/PFAS particulates.

04

Longevity

A "safe" pan that only lasts a month is waste. We perform standardized mechanical abrasion tests.
